Canon Part 1

Up your photography game with the EOS Rebel T7.

Perfect for beginners, this camera bundle offers the essential tools you need to take your SLR skills to new heights — all in one convenient package.

No matter where your next photography adventure takes you, count on the EOS Rebel T7's impressive 24.1 Megapixel CMOS sensor and wide ISO range of 100-6400 (H:12800) to capture high-quality images, even in low-light situations.

Canon Part 2

Priced affordably at $500 — including the Canon EF-S 18-55mm IS II lens — the T7 offers good picture quality and ease of use for those new to DSLRs. At the same time, the T7's feature set is expansive enough for those who want to grow their photography skills.

External controls provide one-touch access to the most important shooting parameters, such as ISO, white balance, focus and drive modes.

The camera's pop-up flash covers a distance of up to 30 feet, but it's most effective if your subject is within 15 feet. If you need more light, you may want to pick up an external flash.
